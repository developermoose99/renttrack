🏘️
Core
Tenant Management, Reimagined
Add tenants with rich profiles — name, unit, rent amount, phone, description, status, and notes. Sort by name, rent, due date, total paid, or status. Search instantly. View detailed payment history per tenant with one click.
💳
Core
Smart Payment Logging
Log payments in seconds. Auto-calculates the next due date based on months covered. Quick-pick chips for 1, 2, 3, 6, or 12-month extensions. Full payment history with running totals.
🔔
New
Dual Notification System
Get reminded both ways — in-app banners appear when rent is due or overdue, and browser push notifications fire even when RentTrack isn't open. Never miss a payment again.
📅
New
Interactive Payment Calendar
Full month-view calendar showing every due date and payment logged. Color-coded overdue, due-soon, and paid days. Click any date for a tenant breakdown popup. Navigate forward and back through months.
📊
Core
Live Dashboard & Charts
At-a-glance stats — total tenants, paid-up count, monthly expected income, and overdue count. Bar chart of rent by unit group, donut chart of payment status, recent activity feed, and upcoming due dates.
📑
New
Excel Report Generation
Export your data three ways — by tenant (one sheet per tenant, full payment history), by month (all tenants for that month), or a full data export with Tenants + Payments sheets. Downloads instantly.
☁️
Core
Real-Time Cloud Sync
Powered by Firebase. Every change syncs instantly across every device. Open RentTrack on your phone and laptop at the same time — both update live. No refresh needed, no data loss.
👥
Core
Multi-User with Roles
Create as many accounts as you need. Assign Admin (full access) or Viewer (read-only) roles. Promote or demote users anytime from the Users page. Each user has their own secure login.
⏱️
New
Configurable Auto-Logout
Set your session timeout from 5 to 60 minutes with a simple slider — or disable it entirely. A 60-second warning appears before logout so you can stay logged in with one click. Keeps your data secure.
